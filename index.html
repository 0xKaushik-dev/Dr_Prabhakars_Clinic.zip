Common Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While appointments are recommended to minimize waiting time, we do accept walk-in patients during our clinic hours (8 PM - 10 PM).

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash and all major UPI payment apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm). We currently do not process insurance claims directly at the clinic.

Do you provide vaccinations for children?

Yes, Dr. Prabhakar provides consultation for routine immunizations. Please call ahead to ensure specific vaccines are in stock.
