Dr. Prabhakar Sharma

Dr. Prabhakar Sharma is a highly respected Family Physician dedicated to providing personalized care for patients of all ages. With a special interest in child diseases, he ensures that the youngest members of your family receive gentle and effective treatment.

Common Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While appointments are recommended to minimize waiting time, we do accept walk-in patients during our clinic hours.

What are your consultation timings?

We are open in the evenings from 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Saturday.

Do you treat infants?

Yes, Dr. Prabhakar specializes in child diseases and provides comprehensive care for infants, including vaccinations.

How do I handle an emergency outside clinic hours?

For emergencies outside clinic hours, please visit the nearest hospital immediately. You can also try calling the clinic number for urgent advice if available.

Do you offer online video consultations?

Yes, we offer online video consultations for follow-ups and minor ailments. Please contact us via phone to schedule a virtual appointment.
